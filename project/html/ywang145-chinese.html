Cuisine Category

Chinese Cuisine

Chinese cuisine is an important part of Chinese culture, which includes cuisine originating from the diverse regions of China.
The most praised "Four Major Cuisines" are Chuan, Lu, Yue and Huaiyang.

Korean Cuisine

Korean cuisine is the customary cooking traditions and practices of the culinary arts of Korea.
It is largely based on rice, vegetables, and meats.

Japanese Cuisine

Japanese cuisine encompasses the regional and traditional foods of Japan, which is based on rice with miso soup and other dishes.
Side dishes often consist of fish, pickled vegetables, and vegetables cooked in broth. Seafood is common, often grilled, but also served raw as sashimi or in sushi.
